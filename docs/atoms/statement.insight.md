# `statement.insight`

## Purpose

A `statement.insight` atom is a conceptual or heuristic statement.

Use when:

- the statement provides intuition or strategy
- the content is conceptual rather than a formal claim

Examples:

- `The center should be viewed as a geometric shadow of Hamiltonian reduction.`
- `This phenomenon suggests a hidden Poisson structure.`

## Guidance

- the body should express interpretation, perspective, or heuristic structure
- it should not be phrased as a theorem-like claim unless it is really a proposition or conjecture
