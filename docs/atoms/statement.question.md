# `statement.question`

## Purpose

A `statement.question` atom is a mathematical inquiry or problem.

Use when:

- the statement is explicitly interrogative
- the note expresses uncertainty or an open problem

Examples:

- `Does the quantum Frobenius determine the center uniquely?`
- `Is this construction functorial?`

## Guidance

- the body should read as a genuine question or problem statement
- it should not be phrased as a finished claim
