# `process.reference_note`

## Purpose

A `process.reference_note` atom records information extracted from external literature.

Use when:

- summarizing a theorem from a paper
- recording an external construction
- preserving a citation-based observation

Examples:

- `Sklyanin proves centrality of the quantum determinant in this setting.`
- `De Concini-Kac describe the large center at roots of unity.`
