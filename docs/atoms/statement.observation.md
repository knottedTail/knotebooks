# `statement.observation`

## Purpose

A `statement.observation` atom is a descriptive or local statement derived from inspection, examples, or computation.

Use when:

- the statement reports a noticed pattern or behavior
- the claim is local, descriptive, or empirical in nature

Examples:

- `In the rank-one case, the center appears finite over its base.`
- `This example resembles the classical situation.`

## Guidance

- weaker than a proposition in force
- often benefits from `supports` links to computations or examples
