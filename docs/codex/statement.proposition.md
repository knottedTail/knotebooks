# Codex Extraction Contract For `statement.proposition`

Emit `type: proposition` when the source presents an assertive mathematical claim intended to be true or false.

Use when:

- the note states a definite claim
- the claim is presented as something provable
- the source label is proposition, theorem, lemma, or corollary

Do not use when:

- the body is explicitly speculative: use `conjecture`
- the body is interrogative: use `question`
- the body is mainly heuristic: use `insight`
