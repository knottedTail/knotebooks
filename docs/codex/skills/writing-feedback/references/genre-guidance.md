# Genre Guidance

Adjust the review standard to the writing genre.

## Daily log

- Keep the writer's personal working voice.
- Prefer light restructuring over heavy rewriting.
- Focus on clarity, grammar, and easier scan-ability.

## Diary-style note

- Preserve reflective tone.
- Fix grammar and awkward phrasing without flattening personality.
- If mathematics appears, distinguish personal reflection from technical content.

## Math research log

- Preserve exploratory language such as uncertainty, guesses, and open questions.
- Make conjectures, observations, failed attempts, and next steps easy to distinguish.
- Encourage explicit notation and short sentences around technical claims.

## Formal mathematical writing

- Raise the bar for precision and structure.
- Prefer concise, declarative sentences.
- Push for explicit hypotheses, notation setup, and logical sequencing.

## General English writing

- Focus on grammar, naturalness, clarity, and tone.
- Avoid adding mathematical critique when none is needed.
