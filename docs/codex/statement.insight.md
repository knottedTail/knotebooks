# Codex Extraction Contract For `statement.insight`

Emit `type: insight` when the source gives an interpretation, perspective, or heuristic idea rather than a formal claim.

Use when:

- the note provides intuition or strategy
- the content is conceptual in force

Do not use when:

- the statement is intended as a definite mathematical claim: use `proposition` or `conjecture`
- the content is mainly a question: use `question`
