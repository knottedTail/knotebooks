# Codex Extraction Contract For `statement.observation`

Emit `type: observation` when the source reports a descriptive, local, or empirical finding.

Use when:

- the note records a pattern noticed from examples or computation
- the claim is weaker than a theorem-like assertion

Do not use when:

- the source is clearly proposing a general formal claim: use `proposition` or `conjecture`
- the content is mostly conceptual interpretation: use `insight`

Extraction notes:

- remove note-local staging phrases unless they materially limit the observation
